What is Egg?

Egg is a place for science stories to nest.

because we belive that even the most mechanical of machines
have the most human of stories to tell.
Anyone can submit an Egg:

all you have to do is upload pictures and text.
in future iterations, we hope to connect scientists and storytellers
for really good Eggs.
