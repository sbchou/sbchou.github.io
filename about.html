Hello, my name is Sophie

I'm a second year Master's student at the MIT Media Lab, in the Laboratory for Social Machines under the supervision of Deb Roy. Previously, I completed a Bachelor's at Columbia University in New York in Computer Science, with a focus in Machine Learning, and was on the Editorial Board of the Columbia Daily Spectator.

My primary interest is in telling stories: whether that is through the words I write or the models I design. I am particularly interested in issues of gender, race, and ethics in algorithmic design.

I'm currently working on a project concerning large scale analysis of the news and conversation around the upcoming U.S. elections.

I'm looking for full-time opportunities in data storytelling and reporting. Please message me on Twitter for any leads.
