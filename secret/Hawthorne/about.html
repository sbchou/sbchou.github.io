Hello, My Name is Sophie

I'm currently a Master's student at the MIT Media Lab, in the Laboratory for Social Machines under the supervision of Deb Roy. Previously, I completed a Bachelor's at Columbia University in New York in Computer Science, with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and was on the Editorial Board of the Columbia Daily Spectator.

My interests center around language: both in computational analysis and expanding the boundaries of communication in art. I am interested in everything from analysing conversations on social networks to personally gaining a deeper understanding of poetry.

I'm currently working on a project concerning large scale analysis of the news and conversation around the upcoming U.S. elections.
